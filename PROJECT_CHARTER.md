# Atlas Project Charter

Version: 0.1.0

Status: Active

---

# Mission

Atlas exists to understand, design, build, benchmark, and operate modern AI infrastructure from first principles.

The project is both an engineering learning journey and a production-grade platform that evolves from a single GPU into a scalable AI inference platform.

---

# Vision

Build an open engineering handbook and platform that demonstrates how modern AI infrastructure is designed and operated.

Atlas should eventually serve three purposes:

- Learning platform
- Engineering portfolio
- Business foundation

---

# Goals

Atlas aims to:

- Learn AI Infrastructure deeply rather than superficially.
- Build every major component whenever practical.
- Benchmark technologies instead of relying on marketing claims.
- Document engineering decisions.
- Think like infrastructure companies rather than application developers.

---

# Non Goals

Atlas is NOT intended to:

- Train frontier foundation models.
- Compete directly with hyperscalers.
- Build consumer AI applications.
- Become another collection of tutorial notes.

---

# Guiding Principles

## Learn from first principles.

Understand why technologies exist before learning how to use them.

---

## Build whenever possible.

Every important concept should have an accompanying hands-on implementation.

---

## Benchmark everything.

Engineering decisions should be backed by measurements whenever possible.

---

## Document decisions.

Every important architectural decision should be recorded using Architecture Decision Records (ADR).

---

## Prefer simplicity.

Choose the simplest architecture that solves today's problem while allowing future growth.

---

## Think like a platform engineer.

Every discussion should answer:

- Why does this technology exist?
- How does it work?
- Why would Atlas need it?
- How would companies use it?

---

# Success Criteria

Atlas Version 1.0 should contain

- Production-quality documentation
- Hands-on labs
- Benchmarks
- Architecture diagrams
- AI inference platform
- Kubernetes deployment
- Multiple inference engines
- Engineering decision records

---

# Engineering Philosophy

Knowledge alone is insufficient.

Every module should produce:

Knowledge

↓

Experiment

↓

Measurement

↓

Engineering Decision

↓

Implementation

↓

Documentation

---

# Long-Term Vision

Atlas should evolve into an open engineering handbook and an extensible AI infrastructure platform capable of serving real-world workloads.
